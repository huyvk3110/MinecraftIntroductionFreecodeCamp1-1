Minecraft

One of the most famous video games of all time

Minecraft is an open world game. A sandbox game allow player to take control and serious impact on the world overall

Minecraft received critical acclaim and won numerous awards, and has been described as one of the most influential and greatest video games in history. Social media, parodies, adaptations, merchandise, and the MineCon convention played large roles in popularizing the game. It has also been used in educational environments, especially in the realm of computing systems, as virtual computers and hardware devices have been built in it. It is the single best-selling video game of all time, selling over 176 million copies across all platforms by late 2019, while having over 112 million monthly active players. A number of spin-off games have also been developed, such as Minecraft: Story Mode, Minecraft Earth, and Minecraft Dungeons.

Here's time life of Minecraft line

  • 17 May 2009 - Minecraft was first released
  • 28 June 2010 - The first major update, dubbed alpha version, was released
  • 11 December 2010 - Persson announced that Minecraft was entering its beta testing phase
  • 18 November 2011 - Mojang moved the game out of beta and released the full version
  • 1 December 2011 - Jens "Jeb" Bergensten took full creative control over Minecraft, replacing Persson as lead designer
  • 28 February 2012 - Mojang announced that they had hired the developers of the popular server platform "Bukkit"[62] to improve Minecraft's support of server modifications
  • 15 September 2014 - Microsoft announced a $2.5 billion deal to buy Mojang, along with the ownership of the Minecraft intellectual property
  • 18 September 2017 - The original version of the game was renamed to Minecraft: Java Edition
